AStar Pathfinder Texture Painter

  • General Use
    • Cloned from: Texture Painter
      • Requires 256x256 textures in the Textures directory.
      • Size, shape, texture of the Brush can be selected. Mouse an area to paint, after loading the Texture.
    • Load the Texture, paint it, then save it. Any textures in the Texture folder are automatically loaded and available
