NanClouds Things

  • Top Portion
    • Select the Size of the Image. Changing the Image size to larger than 128 (256 or 512) may allow cropping the image to select a portion for use. Smaller (64x64) allows pasting into an image.
  • Middle
    • [Clear] Clears the Image to all Black. As these are additive, they need to Start from 0. It is Not automatically reset to all black to allow 'Mixing' the effects.
    • Effects: CA, DLA, C2, C3, NA
      • CA Creates spots.. Rings of lightness. Move the Mouse and click to change the center... rapidly becomes chaotic.. Smoothing in the Texture Editor creates blurry clouds.
      • DLA grows particles from the center... To do: Create a Dilation option to expand the area shape from linear.
      • C2 creates linear-circle spots.. (from upper left to lower right) randomly over the image to build clouds. Moving the Slider changes the Diameter of the spots.
      • C3 creates circle spots.. randomly over the image to build clouds. Moving the Slider changes the Diameter of the spots.
      • NA is a place holder that does nothing; except stop processing what was running.
  • Bottom
    • Quickly adjusting the Slider make C2 and C3 interesting. You can mix and match types without pressing the [Clear] button.
    • [Stop] stops the selected process.
    • [Save] Button
      • The image can be saved used the Save button. Numbering the images (for example: a1..a16 or a1..a24, b1..b16) will aid selection into the texture combiner and keep the images in groups.
    • ? Help got you here
    • [Exit] closes the form
