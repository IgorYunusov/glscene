GLScene MFClouds

  • General Use
    • Press the Go button to make a new image.
      • The checkbox and Slider control the Blackness
      • The Edges button turns the edge black
    • ...
    • The image can be saved used the Save Image button. Changing the Image size to larger than 128 (256 or 512) may allow cropping the image to select a portion for use.A 64x64 image can be 'pasted' into a 128x128 image. Numbering the images (for example: a1..a16 or b1..b16) will aid selection into the texture combiner and keep the images in groups.

MFClouds Things

  • Top Portion
    • [Clear] paints the image black.. no real use.
    • ? Help got you here
    • [GO] Creates a new image IAW the Checkbox and the Slider
      • Checkbox Toggles the use of Blackness level
      • The Slider changes Blackness level from 1 (not enough) to 10 (most of the image) 2 to 4 or 5 is usefull.
    • [Edges] Button will Blacken the edges
    • "Save" Button activates a file Save dilog to name the Image prior to saving it
      • ...
  • Bottom
    • Clicking the Image will 'Paint' with Black or White
      • Right or Left determines White or Black. If it gets too white it starts getting darker. It takes some practice to be usefull
    • Radius changes the area
    • Depth is the intensity..amount of 'color' changed
    • Box or Circle is the shape
