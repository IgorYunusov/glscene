Samples/Demos of VXScene using with Delphi & C++Builder

It's supposed that for all this demos there are next directories:   
- sample projects in $(VXSCENEDIR)\Samples\
- media files in $(VXSCENEDIR)\Samples\media\
- CPP header files in $(VXSCENEDIR)\include\$(Platform)
- CPP lib/bpi files in $(VXSCENEDIR)\lib\$(Platform)

Additional Dynamic libraries, dll with Setup.bat are in the folder $(VXSCENEDIR)\external\

All demos are released under Mozilla PL (MPL).
